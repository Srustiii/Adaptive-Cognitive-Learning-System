Understanding Your Cognitive Mastery Score

The AdaptiveLearn AI platform utilizes a Bayesian Proficiency Estimator modeled using Beta probability distributions.

Mastery Percentage Represents the system's estimated probability that you will successfully solve a challenge at standard difficulty. It rises with correct answers and speed, adapting to harder topics as you progress.
Model Uncertainty Margin Quantifies the statistical variance of the probability distribution. The more activities you complete, the lower the uncertainty becomes, locking in a precise diagnosis of your mastery levels.
